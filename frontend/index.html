Scrap Insights: India & Maharashtra

Facts, figures, and the complete journey from waste to wealth.

Advantages of Scrap Recycling

  • Conserves natural resources like timber, water, and minerals by replacing raw material extraction.
  • Saves up to 95% energy for metals like aluminium compared to mining new ore.
  • Significantly reduces greenhouse gas emissions and carbon footprint across the supply chain.
  • Creates local jobs and boosts circular economy, especially in Maharashtra's 3.5 MT/year scrap industry.
  • Diverts waste from landfills, extending their lifespan and preventing groundwater pollution.

Disadvantages to Address

  • Improper e-waste disposal causes soil and water contamination with toxic heavy metals.
  • Informal scrap workers face health hazards with no protective equipment or safety standards.
  • Mixed and unsorted scrap is labor-intensive to process and reduces material recovery rates.
  • Transportation over long distances for processing adds both cost and emissions.
  • Fluctuating scrap prices make it unreliable income for small-scale collectors.

Scrap-to-Product Transformation Workflow

See how raw scrap travels through the recycling chain and becomes a useful, everyday product.

Plastic Scrap (PET)
🗑️ CollectionPET bottles from households & streets
🔄 ShreddingCleaned & shredded into PET flakes
🧵 SpinningMelted into polyester fibre for textiles
Final: T-shirts, Eco-bricks, Packaging
Metal Scrap (Steel)
🔩 CollectionOld cars, appliances & construction scrap
⚡ SmeltingMelted in electric arc furnace at 1600°C
🏗️ CastingCast into ingots, rods & beams
Final: Rebar, car parts, bridges, appliances
E-Waste (Electronics)
📱 CollectionOld phones, TVs, PCBs & batteries
🔬 DismantlingManual & robotic disassembly
⚗️ RefiningChemical extraction of Gold, Silver, Palladium
Final: New PCBs, jewellery, semiconductors
Paper & Cardboard
📦 CollectionNewspapers, cartons & office waste
💧 PulpingSoaked in water to create pulp slurry
📝 PressingDried & pressed into new sheets
Final: Notebooks, packaging, tissue paper
